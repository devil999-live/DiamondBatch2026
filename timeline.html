📅 April 2025

The first month of Class 10 started. New books, new teachers and the first reminder that **Board Exams are coming**. But our class was still full of fun and excitement.

📅 May 2025

Summer heat and slow classes. Some students started studying seriously while others were still enjoying classroom jokes and fun conversations.

📅 June 2025

Homework slowly increased and teachers became more serious. Still, every day in school was filled with laughter and timepass with friends.

📅 July 2025

Rainy season memories 🌧️ Wet playground, funny moments in corridors and random classroom fun.

📅 August 2025

Independence Day celebrations 🇮🇳 School events, performances, decorations and lots of photos. One of the happiest months of the session.

📅 September 2025

Half-yearly exams approaching. A little tension started appearing but the class still managed to keep the fun alive.

📅 October 2025

Festival season 🎉 Rangoli, celebrations and holidays. For a moment everyone forgot about studies.

📅 November 2025

Teachers started reminding us more often: “Boards are near.” Tests increased and preparation slowly became serious.

📅 December 2025

Winter mornings ❄️ Cold classrooms but warm friendships. Lots of discussions about exams and future plans.

📅 January 2026

Pre-boards arrived and the **real pressure began**. Everyone started studying seriously but our class humor never stopped.

📅 2 February 2026

Our **last regular class of Class 10**. A strange feeling knowing that these daily moments would soon become memories.

🎓 11 February 2026 — Farewell Day

The most emotional day. Photos, speeches, laughter and a few silent tears. The end of our school chapter but the beginning of lifelong memories.
